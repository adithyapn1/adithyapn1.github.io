I’m a researcher working at the intersection of photonics, soft matter, and quantum. I study liquid-crystal topological textures (torons, skyrmions), speckle/sub-Rayleigh imaging, and electrically driven transport in reconfigurable LC devices.

Previously: University of Oxford (Soft Matter Photonics), projects on hybrid 1PP+2PP polymer networks, DHM speckle theory, and toron racetrack prototypes.
